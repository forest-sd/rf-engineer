Our Process

A disciplined engineering approach from requirements to validation.

1. Requirements Analysis

We begin with a thorough analysis of your technical requirements, operational constraints, regulatory needs, and performance objectives.

2. System Architecture

Based on requirements, we develop the RF system architecture including block diagrams, interface definitions, and performance budgets.

3. Detailed Design

Rigorous modeling and simulation drive the detailed design phase, optimizing every element for performance, compliance, and reliability.

4. Design Verification

Comprehensive analysis and simulation verify the design meets all requirements before moving to implementation or prototyping.

5. Documentation

Complete engineering documentation captures design intent, analysis results, specifications, and operational parameters.

6. Support & Review

We provide implementation support, testing guidance, and design review throughout the build and deployment phases.

Signal Integrity & Performance Simulation

Comprehensive signal integrity analysis and performance simulation to ensure reliable system operation.

Signal integrity issues can undermine even the best-designed RF systems. Our signal integrity service uses rigorous analysis and simulation to identify and resolve potential problems before they impact performance, ensuring clean signal paths and reliable data transfer.

Key Capabilities

  • Signal path analysis and optimization
  • Crosstalk and coupling assessment
  • Power integrity analysis
  • Eye diagram and jitter analysis
  • Transmission line characterization
  • EMI/EMC pre-compliance evaluation

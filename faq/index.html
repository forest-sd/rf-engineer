Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our RF engineering services.

What types of RF engineering services do you provide?

We provide comprehensive RF engineering services including system architecture, wireless communication modeling, antenna design and optimization, signal integrity analysis, frequency planning, design validation, engineering documentation, and custom RF solutions.

What industries do you serve?

We serve technology and product development firms, system integrators, infrastructure providers, R&D teams, equipment manufacturers, and any organization requiring advanced RF and wireless engineering expertise.

Do you provide independent design reviews?

Yes, independent technical review is one of our core services. We provide objective assessment of existing RF designs to verify performance, identify risks, and confirm regulatory compliance.

What simulation and modeling tools do you use?

We use industry-standard tools for electromagnetic simulation, propagation modeling, circuit simulation, and signal integrity analysis. Specific tools are selected based on the project's requirements and complexity.

Can you help with regulatory compliance?

Yes, we provide regulatory-aware design support including FCC, CE, and other compliance frameworks. We help ensure designs meet applicable emissions, immunity, and safety requirements.

How do you handle confidential or proprietary projects?

We maintain strict confidentiality protocols and are happy to work under NDA. Protecting client IP and proprietary information is fundamental to our practice.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary by project scope and complexity. A focused analysis might take days, while a full system design could span weeks or months. We provide detailed schedule estimates during project scoping.

Do you work with startups as well as established companies?

Absolutely. We work with organizations of all sizes, from early-stage startups developing their first RF product to established companies needing specialized expertise for complex projects.

Where are you located?

We're located at 800 W El Camino Real, Suite 180 in Silicon Valley, CA 94043. We serve clients throughout the United States and internationally.

How do I get started?

Contact us to discuss your project requirements. We'll review your needs, provide a scope of work, and develop a tailored approach to address your RF engineering challenges.
